02 / Brief

For the fastest response, send these.

Every project starts with a short Discovery Call. To make the first response useful — and the call shorter — drop a few details when you reach out:

— What's the project? A new brand from scratch, a rebrand, or just a logo? Any existing brand assets I should see?

— Why now? What's pushing the timing — a launch, a pivot, a market change?

— Audience. Who's the brand for, and what do you want them to feel?

— Timeline & budget range. Even a rough range helps me know if we're a fit before either of us spends more time.

— References. 2–3 brands you admire (or want to differentiate from) — visual or strategic.

— How did you find me? Behance, LinkedIn, a referral, somewhere else?
