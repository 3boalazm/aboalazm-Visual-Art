02 / How I think

Four principles I refuse to compromise.

01

Strategy before design

No logo until the positioning, audience, and personality are agreed in writing. Without strategy, any logo can be right or wrong — there's no way to judge.

02

Systems, not artifacts

A logo is the entry. The real work is the system that follows — color, typography, voice, templates — so the brand stays itself across every touchpoint.

03

Show the work

Mock the brand into real applications early. A logo on a white page is theory; a logo on a business card, a package, a screen — that's the test.

04

Deliver, don't dump

Final files come with brand guidelines, a handover call, and 30 days of support. The job isn't done when the design is done — it's done when the team can run with it.
