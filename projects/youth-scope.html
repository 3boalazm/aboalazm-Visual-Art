01 / The Brief

A new season, a new identity.

Each Youth Scope season starts with a refreshed identity to reflect that year's theme and program. As Head of Media, my job was to lead a small team through the full identity sprint and ship the campaign assets — twice, across two consecutive seasons.

📸 Season campaign hero · 21:9
02 / The Approach

Lead the process, then the work.

Heading a media team meant I had to be both designer and director — running brand audits, briefing the team, reviewing layouts, and stepping into the work where it counted. The season was the deadline, and the deadline was non-negotiable.

What was delivered each season:

  • Full brand audit of the previous season's gaps
  • New seasonal palette, typography, and identity system
  • Complete campaign rollout: social, recruitment posters, event signage
  • Team direction and review across multiple designers
  • Adobe Suite training workshops for the broader media team

03 / Outcome

Recognized — twice.
