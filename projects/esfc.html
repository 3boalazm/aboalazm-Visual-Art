Education · Field Camp · 2020

Egypt SEG —
Field Camp identity.

A complete visual identity for an annual student field camp — built on a deadline, designed to scale across digital and on-site signage, and credited with driving record participant turnout.

Client

Event

Role

Services

01 / The Brief

A camp that needed a face.

The Egypt SEG Field Camp brings together earth-science students for an intensive practical program. Each year had a different look, no continuity, and registrations were declining. The 2020 brief was simple: give the camp a real identity that would recruit, not just decorate.

📸 Camp identity hero · 21:9
02 / The Approach

Build the system, not just the assets.

Instead of designing each piece individually, I started with a tight visual system — a confident logo, a palette pulled from the desert geology environment, and a typographic hierarchy that worked on both a phone screen and a printed banner.

Deliverables shipped:

  • Primary logo + secondary lockups + monogram
  • Color palette + typography pairing + iconography
  • Full social content kit (announcements, countdowns, daily updates)
  • Banners, roll-ups, ID cards, certificates of participation
  • Email templates and registration form visuals

03 / Outcome

Record turnout.

The 2020 edition saw the camp's highest registration numbers to date. Beyond the numbers, the visual system was carried into the following year's edition — a strong signal that the work had outlasted its deadline.
